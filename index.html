ERASMUS+ INTERNSHIP

    Students are welcome to apply for an internship at Vilnius Gediminas Technical University.

    APPLICATION PACKAGE BEFORE SELECTION PROCEDURE:

  • Motivation letter;
  • Curriculum Vitae;
  • Copy of your passport or ID (must be valid for at least 3 months after the conclusion of your placement in Lithuania)


And other documents, if the company/organization requires.


All documents must be completed electronically, as required. The application package (pdf format) must be sent to the VILNIUS TECH International Relations Office by email internship@vilniustech.lt.
Incomplete application packages will not be accepted.


Please note: we require a minimum of 1 month to process your application package and to explore possible placement for you.

Erasmus+ Student Charter highlights your rights and obligations and tells you what you can expect from your sending and receiving organisations at each step of your Erasmus+ experience.



The VILNIUS TECH International Relations Office or a Lithuanian company will contact you by email with the final decision in the form of either an invitation letter or a refusal letter.

APPLICATION PACKAGE BEFORE SELECTION PROCEDURE Application documents after the selection procedure, ONLY if the candidature will be confirmed by the host company:
Motivation letter Confirmation of Placement Nomination from Your Home Institution (including information about the period of placement and scholarship)
Curriculum Vitae Training agreement (VILNIUS TECH form)
Copy of your passport or ID (must be valid for at least 3 months after the conclusion of your placement in Lithuania)
